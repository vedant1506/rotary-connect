NGO: AIMS AND OBJECTIVES:
• This NGO has three major goals:
1) Service Provision : signiﬁcant goal of rotary club is to provide services and
support to vulnerable or marginalized communities. These services can include
humanitarian aid, healthcare, education, vocational training, disaster relief, and
community development programs.
2) increase Access to Quality Education : This aim focuses on ensuring that all
individuals, especially those from marginalized and disadvantaged communities,
have equal opportunities to receive a quality education. By addressing barriers to
access and promoting inclusive educational practices, Rotary clubs strive to bridge
the educational gap and empower individuals with knowledge and skills for personal
and societal development.
3) Improve Healthcare Access and Quality: This aim centers around ensuring that
all individuals, regardless of their socio-economic background or geographic location,
have access to essential healthcare services and receive high-quality medical care.
Rotary club implements various programs and initiatives, such as establishing health
clinics, providing medical supplies and resources, conducting health awareness
campaigns, and supporting healthcare professionals' training and capacity-building
eﬀorts. Ultimately, the goal is to promote better health outcomes, prevent diseases,
and improve the overall well-being of communities they serve.
